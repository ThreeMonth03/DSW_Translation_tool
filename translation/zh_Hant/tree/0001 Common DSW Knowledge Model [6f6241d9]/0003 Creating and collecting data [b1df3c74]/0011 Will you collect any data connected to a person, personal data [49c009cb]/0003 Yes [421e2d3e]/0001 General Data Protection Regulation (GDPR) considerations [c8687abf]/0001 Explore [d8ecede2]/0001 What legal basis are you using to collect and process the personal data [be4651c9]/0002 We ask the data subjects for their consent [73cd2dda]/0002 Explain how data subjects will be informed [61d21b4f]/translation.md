## text

### Source (en)

~~~text
Explain how data subjects will be informed and how data subject's requests will be handled.

Data subjects must be informed of the purpose, the interest of the study, the type of collected data, and the potential risks to which they will be exposed.

If the processing operation is exempted from the right to information according to the GDPR, provide a justification.
Mention the relevant document in which the responsibilities regarding the information of data subjects are indicated.
~~~

### Translation (zh_Hant)

~~~text
請說明將如何告知資料主體（data subjects）相關資訊，以及如何處理他們所提出的請求。

資料主體必須被告知研究目的、研究的利益、所蒐集的資料類型，以及他們可能承受的潛在風險。

若該資料處理活動依據《一般資料保護規則》（GDPR）可免除告知義務，請提供相關說明。
並請指出載明資料主體告知責任之相關文件。
~~~
