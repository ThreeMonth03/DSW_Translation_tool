### Source (en)

~~~text
Where a type of processing is likely to result in a high risk to the rights and freedoms of natural persons, the controller shall, prior to the processing, carry out an assessment of the impact of the envisaged processing operations on the protection of personal data, a so called Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA) - [Article 35](https://gdpr-info.eu/art-35-gdpr/). Note: Most types of research projects on health or genetic data require a DPIA, especially large-scale processing of sensitive data.
~~~
