## text

### Source (en)

~~~text
Processing of certain categories of personal data is not allowed unless there are exemptions in law to allow this. Among these categories (“sensitive data”) are *“‘[...] data revealing racial or ethnic origin, [...] genetic data, [...] data concerning health’”*. Most types of personal data collected in biomedical research will fall under these categories. [Article 9](https://gdpr-info.eu/art-9-gdpr/) (2) lists a number of exemptions that apply, of which *consent* and *scientific research* are most likely to be relevant for research. Please consult with your Data Protection Officer of your organisation.
~~~
